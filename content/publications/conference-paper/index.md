abstract: |
  We perform a comparative study of unsupervised machine learning algorithms — including Gaussian Mixture
  Models (GMM), DBSCAN, and pyUPMASK — for determining stellar membership probabilities in open clusters
  using astrometric and photometric data from the Gaia DR3 catalogue. Our analysis demonstrates the
  relative strengths and limitations of each method for cluster membership association.
