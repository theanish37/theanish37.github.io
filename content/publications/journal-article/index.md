abstract: |
  We present an updated and expanded catalogue of Low-Mass X-ray Binaries (LMXBs) in the Galaxy,
  combining multi-wavelength data from the INTEGRAL hard X-ray observatory and the Gaia astrometric
  mission. This work provides refined positions, optical counterparts, and updated physical parameters
  for a large sample of Galactic LMXBs, enabling new statistical studies of this population.
